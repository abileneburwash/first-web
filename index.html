Maharshi Mehi Das, The Researcher

It is an attempt to search, Maharshi Mehi (Guruji) through his articulations.

The articulations are in the form of poetic prayers, from an experienced researcher’s perspective. It is a sort of ultimate treasure hunting map and a researcher’s focus regarding the definition of God, the path to reach Him, the prayer a researcher should seek and the practices s/he should implement.
